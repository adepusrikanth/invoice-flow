1.2 AI Chat Assistant — Deep Dive

The AI Chat Assistant is the centerpiece of InvoiceFlow’s AI strategy. It serves three roles: customer support agent, invoicing assistant, and business advisor. It is available 24/7 in the dashboard, mobile app, and via WhatsApp/SMS integration.

User: “Hey, create an invoice for Raj’s Restaurant for 50 kg rice at ₹80/kg”



AI Assistant:

  ✔ Identifies client: Raj’s Restaurant (existing client)

  ✔ Creates invoice: 50 x ₹80 = ₹4,000

  ✔ Applies 18% GST automatically (food supplies category)

  ✔ Sets payment terms: Net 15 (based on client history)

  ✔ Generates PDF with your branding

User: “Yes, and remind me if not paid in 10 days”

6.3 Database Sharding Strategy

At 500K+ users, a single PostgreSQL instance cannot handle the write load. InvoiceFlow uses organization-based sharding — each shard contains a subset of organizations, ensuring all data for one business lives on the same shard.

Sharding Strategy: Organization-based



Shard 1: Organizations A-E  (200K orgs, ~10M invoices)

Shard 2: Organizations F-L  (200K orgs, ~10M invoices)

Shard 3: Organizations M-R  (200K orgs, ~10M invoices)

Shard 4: Organizations S-Z  (200K orgs, ~10M invoices)

Shard 5: Enterprise orgs    (1K orgs, ~5M invoices)



Router: Application-level routing based on org_id hash

Cross-shard queries: Aggregation service for global analytics

Migration: Zero-downtime shard splitting using logical replication
